Welcome, inquisitive friends

Sherlock Holmes is one of the most recognized figures in liturature. He is a household name, and has been for over 100 years now. There have been countless adaptations of Arthur Conan Doyle's works featuring the detective, among which are hundreds of plays, movies, and TV shows. Less famous but equally as interesting are the many uses of Sherlock Holmes in other mediums. This site provides info on some of the ways Holmes has been brought to life in video games.

Most Sherlock Holmes video games are adventure games, but within that genre, they vary widely. The earliest games were text based. The early graphical adventures included pixel art, full motion video, and 3D renders. Real 3D followed in the 00s, the Frogwares offering both first person and traditional third person perspectives on the world. Puzzles range from hidden objects, to the traditional ACT on INVENTORY riddles, and Holmesian logical deduction through manipulation of evidence.

I'm most familiar with the Frogwares titles, so that section of the site will fill out first, but I do hope to play and cover other Holmes adventures as well. Enjoy your visit.
